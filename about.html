About Us

We are a team of developers who are very passionate about music. Yes we do. Yes we are audiophiles to the core. We listen to music all the time. Whichever mood. Listen to music when happy. Listen to music when sad. Listen to music when grumpy. Listen to music to have a good night's sleep. Listen to music when studying. Listen to music when exercising. Listen to music when feeling ill. No genre discrimination. From classic to rock to hiphop to pop to indie, we devour every beat. We time everything by the number of songs we listen to. Yes we listen to music to infinity.
